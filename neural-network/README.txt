	others. Classifier is NN with 10 hidden neurons, trained over 10k epoch
	with 1k validation fail cutoff.

	X axis: true positive rate (i.e., rate of detecting the intruder)
	Y axis: false positive rate (i.e., rate of falsely labeling legitimate user as intruder)
